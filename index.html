About Us

CSEC ASTU is a vibrant computer science and technology club at Adama Science and Technology University (ASTU). Open to all departments, we aim to foster innovation and excellence in the tech community through training, competitions, and real-world projects.

Vision

To be a dynamic hub of innovation, collaboration, and excellence in computer science and technology.

Mission

Empowering students to lead and transform the tech industry through practical skills, mentorship, and collaboration.

Values

  • Innovation
  • Collaboration
  • Inclusivity
  • Excellence
  • Integrity

Our Divisions

Competitive Programming Division (CPD)

Focuses on algorithmic problem-solving through contests, tutorials, and inter-university competitions like ICPC.

Capacity Building Division (CBD)

Prepares seminars, tutorials, and community outreach programs to spread awareness about technology.

Development Division

Enhances web and mobile development skills through real-world projects, challenges, and hackathons.

Cybersecurity Division

Focuses on ethical hacking, cybersecurity, and securing digital infrastructures.

Data Science Division

Explores data science, analytics, and machine learning. Coming soon!
